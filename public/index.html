🔒 Furry Kidz POPIA Privacy Notice
Effective date: 13 July 2026 · Version: furrykidz-popia-v1-2026-07-13

Your privacy matters to us. This notice explains what personal information Furry Kidz Pet Services collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

1. Who we are

Furry Kidz Pet Services is the responsible party for personal information collected through this member portal, daycare booking, Doggy Hotel, transport, grooming, pet care, and invoicing workflow.

2. Information we collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number, emergency contact number, address, preferred location, package type, and client account number.
  • Account login details, stored as a secure password hash.
  • Your dog's profile: name, breed, age, weight, notes, photo, vaccination card/image, vaccination date, deworming date, tick/flea treatment date, sterilisation status, food routine, medication needs, and related care instructions.
  • Booking details: service selections, dates, Doggy Hotel check-in/check-out dates, transport pickup/drop-off choices, AM/PM transport windows, notes, booking status, and invoice status.
  • Operational notes needed by authorised staff for collection, delivery, care, safety, billing, and customer support.

3. Why we collect it

We process this information to create and manage your account, assess whether we can safely accept your dog, provide daycare/Doggy Hotel/grooming/transport services, contact you about bookings, handle emergency or welfare matters, prepare invoices and payment records, and comply with legal, tax, safety, and business record obligations.

4. Sharing and third-party services

We do not sell your personal information. We only share or store information where needed to provide the service, including secure hosting/database systems, authorised Furry Kidz staff, accounting/invoicing systems such as Xero where enabled, and where required by law.

5. How long we keep it

We keep active account, pet, booking, and operational records for as long as needed to provide services and maintain safety history. Financial and invoice records may be retained for the period required under South African tax and accounting laws. You may ask us to correct or delete information where lawful and practical.

6. How we protect it

The portal uses HTTPS and access-controlled admin/staff areas. Passwords are stored as hashes, not plain text. Staff access is limited to operational information needed for bookings, care, collections, and deliveries.

7. Your POPIA rights

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Object to processing or request deletion where POPIA allows.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Information Regulator: www.justice.gov.za/inforeg · inforeg@justice.gov.za

By ticking the consent checkbox, you confirm that you have read this notice and consent to Furry Kidz processing the information listed above for account, booking, pet care, safety, transport, invoicing, and support purposes.
